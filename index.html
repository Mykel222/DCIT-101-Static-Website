TOP 3 SENIOR HIGH SCHOOLS IN WESTERN REGION OF GHANA

1. GHANA SECONDARY TECHNICAL SCHOOL

Ghana Secondary Technical School is an all boys school in the western region of Ghana. It is also known as GSTS. GSTS is the first technical school in Ghana. It was established in 1909. GSTS is currently the best school in Western Region of Ghana. Even though it is not performing as expected but it is still regarded as the best school in the Western Region with its rival school being St. Johns School. Based on achievement , it has gotten one NSMQ trophy and keeps on performing in NSMQ than the other western regional schools. It has also provided prominant people in the country. There is no doubt that it is the best school in Western Region. This is the entrance of GSTS

2. ST. JOHNS SCHOOL

St. Johns School is an all boys catholic school in Western Region of Ghana. These boys are normally known as The Saints. It was established on 29th January, 1952 in Ghana. Even though it is one of the best school in Ghana, it is regarded as the second best in Western Region due to the presence of Ghana Secondary Technical Schol. Even though it has also gotten prominant people in the country, the performance of GSTS is superb. As a result, it is regarded as the second best in the country. This is the entrance of St. Johns

3. ARCHBISHOP PORTER GIRLS SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL

Archbishop Porter Girls is an all girls school in the western region of Ghana. It is one of the best girls school in the country. It was established in the year 1965. Eventhough it is prominent in WASSCE rankings in the country, it is regarded as the third best school in the country due to the presence of GSTS and The Saints. This girls school is also known in the country to be producing prominent personnel in the country. This is the entrance of AGPSS
